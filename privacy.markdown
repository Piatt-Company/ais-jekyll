AI Infrastructure Services ("AIS", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information through our website.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, primarily through our contact forms and email communications. This includes:
* **Contact Information:** Name, email address, company name, and phone number.
* **Business Inquiries:** Information regarding your infrastructure needs, RFP details, or site assessment requests.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
* Respond to your inquiries and service requests.
* Process operational assessments and logistics coordination.
* Send administrative information, such as updates to our terms or policies.

## 3. Data Infrastructure

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Contact form submissions are processed securely using Google Apps Script and stored within our corporate Google Workspace environment. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

We use standard cookies to improve website performance and user experience. You can choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.
