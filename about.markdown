## Our Mission

**To professionalize the operations of AI data centers through rigor, specialized training, and unyielding safety standards.**

As compute density increases, the margin for operational error decreases. AIS provides the specialized workforce required to maintain the physical layer of the Intelligence Age.

## Leadership

### Bret Piatt, Founder
Bret leverages extensive experience as an advisor, executive, and investor in the cloud and cybersecurity sectors. 

Previously, he served as **Chief Executive Officer of CyberFortress**, a global cybersecurity company. His career also includes leading a $100+ million SaaS business unit at **Rackspace** and managing **AT&T's** Global Firewall Managed Service.

Bret holds a background in computer science and mathematics from California State University, Chico, and an executive MBA from Quantic School of Business and Technology. Based in San Antonio since 2004, he currently serves on the boards of BioBridge Global, Geekdom, and the Leadership San Antonio Alumni Association. He is also a Director for the Texas Lyceum (President, 2026).
